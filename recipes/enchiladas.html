Enchiladas

Enchiladas! A delicious dish from south of the border, great with beef, chicken, or your favorite choice of protein!

Enchilada Ingridients:

  • 1.25 pounds chicken (or portein of your choice)
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1.5 teaspoons cumin
  • 12 tortillas
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 medium poblano pepper
  • 1 small bell pepper
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 8 ounces cream cheese
  • 4 cups shredded cheese
  • 1 cup refried beans
  • 1 can red enchilada sauce

Enchilada Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F
  2. Place chicken on foil and season
  3. bake in the preheated oven until tender (about 50 minutes)
  4. Toast each tortilla over medium heat in a skillet
  5. Sautee chopped onions and peppers
  6. Add chicken, beans, and sauteed onions and peppers to each tortilla
  7. Arrange tortillas in a single layer and pour enchilada sauce on top
  8. Bake about 30 minutes

Enjoy your enchiladas, hombres!
